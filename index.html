What is Ethereum?

Ethereum is a technology built for the public good.

Ethereum is a global, open-source platform that enables its participants to create and store digital value without relying on any central authority. Trust in central authorities is replaced with trust in mathematics and cryptographic methods.

Ethereum is not controlled by any one entity, and is developed and maintained by a diverse community of individuals, organizations, and users across the globe. It is a shared resource for all to access and utilize. Participants are incentivized to secure this vast network. Access to the Ethereum network is unrestricted and offered as a public good. The network is secured by nodes that can be run by anyone. This global network of nodes work together to maintain a shared state and establish a foundation of trust for the benefit of all.

At ProtocolBlocks our mission is to help secure the Ethereum network as a public good for the benefit of all. Every decision we make is guided by our mission.

Staking Rewards the Easy Way

ProtocolBlocks works with the decentralized Rocket Pool network to create and offer the rETH digital asset. rETH is a liquid staking derivative product, which offers its holders staking rewards from the Ethereum network. In the digital asset space, Ethereum staking yield is considered to be the highest quality, as its yield is generated from the most trusted and secure blockchain network. Simply by holding the rETH token, users are able to earn Ethereum staking rewards without the complexities of running their own validators. Learn more about the rETH digital asset and it's growing number of integrations across the DeFi landscape below:

Rocket Pool

Rocket Pool is a decentralized protocol designed to be community owned, trustless and compatible with staking in Ethereum. They are the creators of the rETH digital asset.

rETH Token

rETH is a liquid staking derivative product, which offers its holders staking rewards from the Ethereum network without the complexities of running a validator.

MakerDAO

MakerDAO is the original protocol that many credit with starting the DeFi movement. MakerDAO accepts the rETH digital asset as collateral for loans in the stablecoin DAI.

Uniswap

Uniswap is one of the most respected and trusted DeFi protocols. Uniswap is a digital asset exchange which offers its users the ability to exchange for the rETH token.

Balancer

Balancer is another reliable and trusted DeFi protocol. Balancer offers users the ability to exchange for the rETH digital asset so that they can start earning Ethereum staking yield.

Aave

Fingers crossed, Aave will soon add support for rETH to its marketplace. By doing so, rETH holders would benefit from the ability to use rETH as collateral to borrow against.
