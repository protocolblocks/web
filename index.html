What is Ethereum?

Ethereum is a technology built for the public good.

Ethereum is a global, open-source platform that enables its participants to create and store digital value without relying on any central authority. Trust in central authorities is replaced with trust in mathematics and cryptographic methods.

Ethereum is not controlled by any one entity, and is developed and maintained by a diverse community of individuals, organizations, and users across the globe. It is a shared resource for all to access and utilize. Participants are incentivized to secure this vast network. Access to the Ethereum network is unrestricted and offered as a public good. The network is secured by nodes that can be run by anyone. This global network of nodes work together to maintain a shared state and establish a foundation of trust for the benefit of all.

At ProtocolBlocks our mission is to help secure the Ethereum network as a public good for the benefit of all. Every decision we make is guided by our mission.

Beneath the Surface

ProtocolBlocks brings together everything you need, from core infrastructure to software, to support the Ethereum network and the future of the decentralized web. Core services provided are listed below. Email Us to learn how you can benefit from our services.

Attestation

Provides a permanent and verifiable record of the validity of blockchain data. Attestation services allows for greater trust and confidence in the Ethereum network.

Block Proposal

Blocks are the fundamental units of the blockchain. ProtocolBlocks proposes new blocks of transactions, to be agreed up by participants in the network.

Sync Committee

Signs and validates block headers, to enable light clients to work with a streamlined version of Ethereum data. This will enable a future of greater decentralization.

Slashing Rewards

Inappropriate conduct by node operators is met with fees. The whistleblowing partipants identifying such inappropriate conduct are entitled to a reward.

Priority Fees

Processes priority fees (tips) from users transacting on the Ethereum network to reward node operators for their services. These fees are optional in nature.

MEV Rewards

Reviews pending transactions and employs sophisticated algorithms to modify the ordering of transactions to extract additional value for nodes maintaining the network.
